1. What QuickPost Does

QuickPost is a social publishing tool (Buffer-like). Users can connect social accounts and publish content to selected platforms on their behalf.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account & identity: Email/name (if provided) and a user ID for app login.
  • OAuth tokens: Access tokens / refresh tokens required to connect and publish.
  • Connected account identifiers: Page IDs, account IDs, business IDs, board IDs (where applicable).
  • User content you provide: Images/videos/captions and related metadata required for publishing.
  • Logs & diagnostics: Basic server logs (errors, timestamps) to keep the app stable and secure.

3. Platforms We Integrate With

Depending on what you connect, QuickPost may interact with these third-party APIs:

  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram) APIs for publishing and insights.
  • YouTube APIs for uploads.
  • Pinterest APIs for creating Pins and managing publishing to boards you select.
  • Bluesky APIs for posting content.

4. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate you and keep your session secure
  • Connect your social accounts and show connection status
  • Publish content only when you request it
  • Show publishing history and results (success/failure)
  • Prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, and improve reliability

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in these cases:

  • With connected platforms: When you publish, QuickPost sends your content and required identifiers/tokens to the platform API.
  • Service providers: We may use infrastructure providers (e.g., hosting, file storage/CDN) to store media temporarily for publishing.
  • Legal obligations: If required by law or valid legal process.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • OAuth tokens are stored securely and used only for authorized actions.
  • We apply reasonable safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access.
  • You should keep your platform accounts secure and use strong passwords/2FA.

7. Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the Service:

  • Tokens: Stored until you disconnect the account or request deletion.
  • Publishing history: Stored to show your post results; you can request deletion.
  • Logs: Kept for a limited period for security and debugging purposes.

8. Your Choices & Controls

  • Disconnect accounts: You can disconnect any platform from the app dashboard. Tokens are removed.
  • Request deletion: You can request full deletion of your stored data (details below).
